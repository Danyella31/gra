Yakisoba Noodles

Yakisoba is a classic Japanese stir-fried noodle dish packed with flavor and colorful vegetables. Perfect as a quick meal or a satisfying dinner, this recipe brings the authentic taste of Japan to your table.

Ingredients

  • 200g yakisoba noodles
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cup sliced carrots
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 100g chicken or Steak
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cut the ingredients into bite-size pieces.
  2. Heat sesame oil in a large pan or wok.
  3. Add chicken or Steak and stir-fry until cooked.
  4. Add the vegetables and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.
  5. Add the cooked yakisoba noodles to the pan.
  6. Stir in soy sauce and Worcestershire sauce. Mix well.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot!
